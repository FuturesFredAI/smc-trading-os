A+
100% WR · Full 1% size
BOTH sweep AND delivery from prior FVG
Instant, strong momentum — NO chop through iFVG
One of the 7 valid targets present
Singular and obvious FVG — not overlapping mess
Correct P/D — longs in discount, shorts in premium
A
62.5% WR · Full 1% size
EITHER sweep OR delivery — not both required
Good momentum — no chop, not necessarily instant
One of the 7 valid targets present
Singular, obvious FVG
Correct P/D positioning. SMT is a bonus.
⚠ A on choppy days = A-
A-
31% WR · 0.5% max
Sweep or delivery present
Some chop — wait for body close fully over/under FVG
One of the 7 targets
May be slightly wrong side of P/D
⚠ Grade inflation risk — be honest
B+
50% WR · 0.25% max
A- quality setup but ES is bad/choppy
Very choppy day where the gap looks super obvious
B / B- / C
0% WR · SKIP
Do not trade. No sweep, no delivery, heavy chop, no real targets, forcing an FVG.
